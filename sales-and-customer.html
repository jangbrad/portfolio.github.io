Simulated IKEA 2023 Sales & Customer Dashboard

Simulated executive summary dashboard highlighting key financial and customer metrics for IKEA 2023. Focused on KPI trends, subcategory analysis and top customers for data-driven decision-making.

Tableau Data Visualisation Sales Analysis Customer Insights Portfolio Case Study

1. Simulated Project Overview & Role

Timeline: 2 days | Stakeholders: VP Sales, Customer Success Director | Role: Lead Data Analyst & Tableau Developer

Designed a simulated Sales & Customer Dashboard to consolidate financial and customer metrics for IKEA 2023. The goal was to highlight trends in sales, profit, customer acquisition and orders, providing executives with actionable insights.

2. Problem Statement / Challenge

IKEA's sales and customer metrics were previously analysed in isolated reports. There was no unified view to track revenue growth, customer behaviour or operational efficiency over time.

  • Fragmented Data Sources: Sales, customer and order data were scattered across multiple systems.
  • Lack of Trend Insights: Leadership could not easily track performance vs previous year or identify growth opportunities.
  • Limited Analytical Depth: Existing reporting lacked granular sub-category, customer and geographic analysis.

3. Methodology & Solution

Data & Tools

Used SQL to clean, transform and aggregate sales, profit, customer and order data before importing it into Tableau. In Tableau, the data was modeled and prepared to simulate an executive summary dashboard featuring key KPIs and trend analysis. Advanced Tableau techniques were applied, including LOD expressions, table calculations with window functions, data preparation and modeling, as well as advanced charting and dashboard design to enable interactive insights.

Key Analytical Steps

  1. Data Consolidation: Combined datasets for Total Sales, Profit, Customers, Orders and Product Categories using SQL to ensure accurate KPI calculation.
  2. KPI Development: Created Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Customers, Sales per Customer and Total Orders as executive-level KPIs with year-over-year comparison, leveraging Tableau calculated fields and LOD expressions.
  3. Trend & Subcategory Analysis: Built line charts for weekly sales, profit and customer trends and bar charts for sales and profit by product subcategory using advanced charting and formatting techniques.
  4. Customer Segmentation: Identified top customers by profit and order frequency using interactive table visualizations, distribution charts and dynamic filters.
  5. Advanced Tableau Features: Applied window functions, parameter-driven calculations, color-coded performance indicators and scenario analysis to enable multi-dimensional insights.
  6. Stakeholder Alignment: Designed dashboards and KPIs to simulate realistic executive reporting standards with analytical rigor expected in senior analyst roles.
