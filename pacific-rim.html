Scopus Scholarly Output & FWCI Analysis

An interactive bubble chart that visualises the scholarly output and Field-Weighted Citation Impact (FWCI) of global universities, highlighting top institutions and benchmarking The University.

Python Plotly Pandas Data Visualisation Higher Education

1. Project Overview & Role

Timeline: 2 weeks | Stakeholders: Associate Directors, Directors and Research International Managers | Role: Lead Data Analyst & Python Developer

This project was designed to create an interactive, data-driven visualiation to explore global university research performance, identify leaders in scholarly output and FWCI and benchmark The University against its peers.

2. Problem Statement / Challenge

Universities generate massive amounts of research data but lack accessible visualisations to benchmark performance globally. Manual reporting was time-consuming and stakeholders could not easily identify top-performing institutions or understand The University's relative position.

  • Data Complexity: Large CSV datasets with inconsistent formatting required cleaning and preprocessing.
  • Benchmarking Gap: No automated comparison for FWCI or scholarly output across global institutions.
  • Visualisation Needs: Stakeholders needed an interactive, intuitive view for decision-making and strategic insight.

3. Methodology & Solution

Data & Tools

Data was extracted from SciVal, a scholarly database and was automatically cleaned and processed in a pipeline with Python using Pandas, Plotly Express and Plotly Graph Objects to produce an interactive bubble chart and a separate legend.

Key Analytical Steps

  1. Data Cleaning: Converted numeric columns, handled missing values and standardised entity names.
  2. Labeling & Highlighting: Automatically labeled top 10 entities by FWCI, top 10 by output and universities near The University.
  3. Visualization: Created bubble chart mapping FWCI vs. scholarly output, bubble size indicating citations in policy documents and custom color mapping for 60+ universities.
  4. Legend Creation: Generated a separate interactive legend for all universities to improve readability.
